About Sniffypedia

The Logo

The working logo of Sniffypedia is that of the "unknown radio device" from a reelyActive pitch deck dating back to the summer of 2013. The relevant slides are below. Have a better idea for a logo? This project is open to contributions!

Where is this hosted?

Sniffypedia is currently hosted on GitHub, as the GitHub Pages branch of reelyActive's sniffypedia repository. The repository, and hence the entire project, can easily be cloned and hosted on any web server, both locally or in the cloud.
